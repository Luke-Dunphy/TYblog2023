TY 2023 Red Hat Blog

Monday

Today was day 1 of the TY Red Hat work experience.It was a great day overall in my opinion, the day started off with all 10 students going to the boardroom to find out what redhat is from Brendan we then got to see are timetable and what we had to do for the week from the person who will be taking care of us for the week Cathal.We then met James who talked about redhat and what the company is and how the make money after that we played a game where you got a sheet taped onto your back and you had to guess the famous person on your back. After that we got a tour of the building from the secratary and this place is massivve.

When we finished the tour we then sat back down at our desks and Cathal put work up onto the board, the work consisted of downloading three softwares Vscode,Python and Git we then had to signup to Github,Trello and Slack once we had signed up to slack we then had to send are username onto Cathal all that work led us up to lunch.

After lunch we had a workshop for two hours where we talked about how to use Git and we also talked about our blogs aswell we also used a software called terminal and getting to know how to use it fully and the codes to put in the software for shortcuts and to know where we actually are.

Tuesday

Today was day 2 of Redhat we started at 9:30 today instead of yesteday satrting at 10:00. The day started off by completing the previous days tasks or working on our blog which lasted until 10:30, we then got talk from Sarah Finn on Agile in this meeting we talked about what is agile,the benefits that you can experience,the history of agile,agile at work,the agile mindset,agile success factors and the slogan that we use Try Rinse Repeat. After the talk or meeting we got given a hour to work on the previous tasks that we hadnt finished or work on our blogs which ended up taking us up to lunch. After lunch we got too have a two hour workshop with Dominik wich I really enjoyed personally. I felt this workshop really helped understand the microbit more and helped me to learn more about coding the microbit.Ive been learning microbit in school for a few weeks but never really understood it but after this workshop with Dominik I feel i understand the microbit more now.After the workshop we only had an hour till we got to go home so me and the other TY students just worked on our blogs and I also had a chat with Cathal about what I though of the workshop and what did I think of the first few days.

Wednesday

@homerj on October 7, 202X

Today was day 3 of Redhat, again we started at 9:30 the same as yesterday.Today was a different day it was more of a relaxed day as the first two days were heavy in work. We started the day off by completing the previous days tasks or working on our blogs for the first hour of the day after that we did some team building workshop where we got to build lego for a hour which was really good after that we got a talk from Rachel Lawton who talked about a day in the life of a software engineer and her back story and on how she didnt take the traditional root into getting hired by Redhat. After the talk we got some free time where we could continue to work on our blog or go for an early lunch whish was pretty good,I wont lie I took the early lunch instead.

After lunch we had a talk with Rachels little brother Daniel Lawton which was about student life,this talk was very funny in all ways as he was talking abouut drinking, the college life,SETU and not knowing what he actually wanted to do for college.He went into great detail on the computing course and on why he thinks SETU is better than any other colleges and to be fair he made some great points and while he was doing that he probably made a fool out of his sister Rachel which was pretty funny, after the talk we were supposed to do another teamwork challenge and the challenge was called the Bridge challenge but Cathal decided not to do the work shop but to let us work on our blogs or let us work on the previous team building challenge which was the lego challenge in all it was a very funny and a very good day.

Thursday

Today was day 4 of Red Hat we started at 9: 30 today,are first bit of work to do today was to complete previous day tasks or work on our blog we done this until 10:00 as the company had a meeting with Waterford Office Town Hall in the boardroom where all the TY students had to to say their name and one intersting fact about them which was good.Brendan was on fire with jokes throughout the meeting which were really funny everyone also had the laugh with Rachel calling her famous as she had an interview with Beat 102-103.After the meeting we all had breakfast in the canteen which was pretty good.

After we had all had breakfast and the meeting we then had up to lunch to work on completing the previous days tasks or working on are blog which was handy because I got time to work on the background of my blog and changing the photos aswell which was good,I also got time to work on adding more boxes so i could add the rest of the week aswell.

After lunch we had a workshop with Christian for two hours where we talked about Python and how to use Pyhton aswell.The workshop was really good,Christian had come up with a game with a few bugs in it and we had to find out what the bugs were and then fix them through VScode.After the workshop we had free time unless you had to finish your blog.
